Recruitment

To form a new unit, you can choose different options.

  1. You may conscript forces from the local population.
    • Pro: it's almost always possible and cheap to conscript units.
    • Con: conscripted units will probably lack proper training.
  2. You may form a professional unit of soldiers.
    • Pro: properly trained and better willingness to fight.
    • Con: more expensive.
  3. You may hire mercenaries.
    • Pro: mostly true battle-hardened fighters.
    • Con: most expensive, sporadic availability.
